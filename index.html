Everything you need to know about Alchemy Market's services and offerings

Alchemy Market is a technology-driven platform designed to support SEBI-registered Research Analysts (RAs), Investment Advisers (IAs), educational professionals, and other financial service providers. We offer digital infrastructure, data tools, and educational content to help them operate efficiently — without offering investment advice ourselves.

No. Alchemy Market does not provide investment advice or fund management. We build and operate the technology backbone that empowers financial educators and licensed professionals to distribute their content, research, and tools legally and efficiently.

We provide a comprehensive suite of services including:

  • Technology platforms for RAs, IAs, and educators
  • Market data integration and analytics tools
  • Financial education content in regional languages
  • Broker referral infrastructure (in line with regulations)
  • Commission-based tools (e.g., affiliate or partnership programs)
  • AI-based research tools and fintech innovation services

Alchemy Market itself is not registered as an RA or IA with SEBI, as it does not provide regulated advisory services. However, we collaborate with SEBI-registered individuals/entities by offering a compliant digital platform that supports their work.

Our comprehensive educational curriculum includes:

  • Stock Market Basics and Fundamentals
  • Technical & Fundamental Analysis
  • Mutual Funds (SIP, NAV, Taxation, Asset Allocation)
  • Financial Ratios and Sector Analysis
  • Market Psychology and Behavioral Finance
  • Risk Management and Portfolio Construction

Our content is designed for a wide range of learners:

  • Beginners with no finance background
  • College students exploring finance careers
  • Retail investors and working professionals
  • Anyone who wants to learn how to analyze the stock market independently
  • Financial professionals seeking to enhance their knowledge
